A WIP Raycast extension that lets you check sunrise / sunset times for dates up to a month from now.

Uses IP geolocation to get your current location, and suncalc.js to calculate sunrise / sunset times.

**Technologies used**  
TypeScript, React  
**Deployed on**
Vercel
